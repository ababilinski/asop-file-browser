FAQ

Frequently asked.

Do I need an app on my phone?

No. It uses connection features already built into your phone.

Is it free?

Yes. ASOP File Browser is free and open source under the GPL v3.

Do I need Developer Options to move files?

No. File Transfer Mode works over USB. Developer Options are only needed for Wi-Fi and Phone Tools.

Can I connect over Wi-Fi?

Yes. On Android 11 or later, turn on Wireless debugging and follow the Wi-Fi steps.

Do I need an account?

No. There is no sign-in or account.

Is it private?

Files move directly between your phone and Mac. There is no account, app analytics, or cloud file transfer. Read the Privacy Policy.

Can I transfer files between devices?

Yes. Move files between your phone and Mac. If two debugging-connected phones appear in the app, you can also copy and paste between them; the transfer is staged locally on your Mac.

What version of macOS does it support?

macOS 13 Ventura or later, on Apple silicon and Intel Macs.

Troubleshooting

When something is off.

Start with the connection, then try the action again.

File Transfer Mode cannot find the phone

Unlock the phone, choose File transfer from its USB notification, then scan again.

The cable charges but nothing appears

Try another cable or USB port. Some cables only carry power.

The debugging connection is waiting or unauthorized

Unlock the phone, reconnect the cable, approve the debugging prompt, then refresh. The USB debugging steps show the full setup.

Wi-Fi pairing will not finish

Keep both devices on the same Wi-Fi network and leave the phone’s pairing screen open. If it still fails, turn Wireless debugging off and back on before pairing again.

Phone Tools are missing or damaged

Open Settings → Tools. Choose Managed Copy for the missing tool, then choose Download. You can also use a compatible copy already on your Mac. See Phone Tools details.

A folder will not open

Android protects some app and system folders. Try Internal Storage, Downloads, Pictures, or another folder Android makes available.

A transfer stopped partway through

Reconnect the phone and use Retry in the transfer panel. Check that the destination still has enough free space.
